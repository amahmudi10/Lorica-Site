How it works

01

Worker speaks

A question, a command, a translation request — spoken naturally into Lorica on their wrist.

02

Phone processes it

Lorica sends the request to the worker's phone. Voice commands, AI responses, and translations all run there — no cloud required.

03

Answer comes back instantly

Audio response through Lorica's speaker. The worker never had to stop, look down, or take off a glove.
